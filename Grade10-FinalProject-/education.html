PTEC is an advanced technology program that allows high school students to learn skills within Software Development and Cybersecurity. They master these skills by participating in hands-on experiences, such as designing websites, making games, creating a network with multiple devices and assembling & disassembling hardware. This program provides a complete enriched experience with many professionals in the technology field coming to speak to the students and providing an interactive mentorship. During their final two years of high school, the PTEC students transfer their learning environment from SMART Park to MITT where they approach a more specific learning curriculum.
